Exporting models from blender to Unity
-------------------------------------------

#blender
#Unity
#Game-dev
#Art

To export a model from blender to Unity, do as follows:
- Go to file -> export == Then choose Wavefront (.obj) in this case
- Change Forward axis to -x and upwards axis to Y
- Press export and place both files in your Unity project
## If you have multiple models, remember to check "selection only"
